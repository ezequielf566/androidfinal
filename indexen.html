How it all began...

I didn’t know much about programming.

But I had a dream — to bring God’s Word to children in a joyful and creative way.

It all started thinking about my son and my sisters, who always loved to color.

That inspired me, and with faith and dedication, the dream became reality!

Today, Coloring the Word reaches families around the world. 🌍💛
